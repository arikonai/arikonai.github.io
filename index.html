Support & Clarity

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Arikon's AI solutions.

What is Arikon? ▾
Arikon is a managed AI infrastructure platform purpose-built for Australian real estate agencies. We automate seller enquiries, lead qualification, and voice interactions — creating a unified operational layer that runs 24/7 across your agency.
How does the AI handle seller enquiries? ▾
When a seller enquiry is received via your website or property portals, Arikon responds instantly with a professional, brand-aligned message. The system qualifies the lead, gathers key property details, scores the opportunity, and routes it to the right agent in real time.
Can it integrate with my existing CRM? ▾
Yes. Arikon integrates seamlessly with major Australian real estate CRMs. All enquiries, conversations, and lead updates are automatically synced, ensuring your team works from a single source of truth.
Is the voice agent natural sounding? ▾
Yes. Our voice agent uses advanced speech technology trained for real estate conversations. It handles inbound calls, answers common questions, qualifies sellers, and books appraisals directly into your calendar — sounding professional and local.
How long does implementation take? ▾
Implementation is fully managed by our team and typically completed within a few days. There is no complex technical setup required from your agency.
Will this replace my agents? ▾
No. Arikon is built to support your agents, not replace them. It handles the initial response, qualification, and routing so your team can focus on high-value conversations, listing presentations, and closing deals.
Is our data secure? ▾
Yes. Arikon operates on secure cloud infrastructure with strict access controls and enterprise-grade security standards. All enquiry data remains owned by your agency and is handled in line with Australian privacy regulations.
Does Arikon work outside business hours? ▾
Yes. Arikon operates 24/7 across your website, property portals, and phone lines. Every enquiry is captured and responded to instantly — even after hours, on weekends, and during peak listing periods.
Can we customise the AI to match our brand? ▾
Absolutely. Arikon is configured to reflect your agency's brand voice, suburb expertise, and appraisal strategy. Every response is professional, on-brand, and aligned with how your team communicates with sellers.
What happens if a lead needs human attention? ▾
Arikon intelligently escalates high-value or complex enquiries to the appropriate agent in real time. Your team is notified immediately, ensuring seamless human follow-up when required.
